Albert Einstein

The man who revolutionized science.

A time line of Albert Einstein's life:

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, Germany
  • 1880 - Moves to Munich
  • 1889 - Becomes student at Luitpold Gymnasium
  • 1894 - Moves to Milan with family
  • 1896 - renouces German citizenship and attends cantonal school in Aarau, Switzerland
  • 1901 - Becomes Swiss citizen
  • 1903 - Marries Mileva Maric
  • 1904 - Albert's son Hans is born
  • 1905 - Receives PhD from University of Zunich and publishes theory of special relativity
  • 1909 - Becomes associate lecturer in Theoretical Physics at University of Zunich
  • 1910 - Albert's son Eduard is born
  • 1912 - Lecture in Theoretical Physics at Federal Technological Institute of Zunich
  • 1914 - Becomes lecturer of University of Berlin
  • 1916 - Published theory of general relativity
  • 1917 - Becomes director of Kasier Wilhelm Institute of Physics in Berlin. Einstein's health begins to detiorate and his cousin Elsa Loewenthal begins taking care of him.
  • 1919 - Divorces his wife Mileva and marries his cousin Elsa. A solar eclipse confrims Einstein's predictions on the direction of light rays. This sky rockets him into world fame.
  • 1922 - Awarded 1921 Nobel Prize for Physics. Publishes his first work on the unified field theory. He aslo becomes a member of the League of Nation's Committee on Intellectual Cooperation, and begins his world tour.
  • 1927 - Einstein had his first debates with Niels Bohr on how quantum mechanics is explained in nature.
  • 1932 - Hitler takes power in Germany and Nazi anti-semitism instensifies.
  • 1933 - Einstein and his family flee to Princeton, USA.
  • 1936 - Einstein's wife Elsa dies.
  • 1939 - World War II statrts and Einstein writes a letter to President Roosevelt warning of the Germany manufacturing an atomic bomb.
  • 1940 - Gains US citizenship
  • 1945 - Atomic bombs dropped in Hiroshima and Nagasaki
  • 1946 - Einstein chairs the Emergency Committee of Atomic Scientists and continues his campaign against re-armament and in favour of world government.
  • 1947 - Mileva dies in Zunich
  • 1952 - Was offered and denied the presidency of Israel
  • 1955 - Einstein dies in Princeton of an aortic aneurysm in the abdominal aorta.
